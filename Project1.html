Nasty Nets Introduction:

Nasty Nets Introductory Information: Nasty Nets was a blog created by an internet surfing club that was active from August 2006-2012. Surf Clubs, a term Nasty Nets themselves coined, was an internet space used to share items across the internet whether it be media pieces or their own work. The group consisted of about thirty nine artists and featured over a thousand posts. These artists were emerging amateurs who used the internet in different ways to express themselves. Artists often featured wacky glitch art when providing their own work, and to encourage one another would comment under each other's posts.
